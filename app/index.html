Elastic Demand

|Ed| > 1

Quantity changes more than price. Lower prices increase total revenue.

Examples: Luxury goods, vacations, restaurant meals

⚖️

Unit Elastic

|Ed| = 1

Quantity changes proportionally with price. Revenue stays constant.

Examples: Some consumer goods at equilibrium

📉

Inelastic Demand

|Ed| < 1

Quantity changes less than price. Higher prices increase total revenue.

Examples: Gasoline, utilities, necessities
